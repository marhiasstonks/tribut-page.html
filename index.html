Elon Musk

The living Iron Man

A time line of some of most important proyects and companies:

  • 1995-Along with his brother, Musk founded Zip2, an online business directory, as an online alternative to the standard paper yellow pages.
  • 1999-Musk invested some of his windfall from the Zip2 sale into his next venture: X.com, an online bank, launched with three other co-founders.
  • 2000-X.com merged with software company Confinity just one year later, forming PayPal, a secure online payments platform with a foundation in payments conducted for PalmPilots.
